OUR HOTEL

A welcoming haven for business travelers, it is easily accessible from all major cities in 15 minutes.a Group of hotels as Colorful as Equity Hotel itself, we combines Trusted Brands, spirited bars & restaurants, and Exceptional accommodations for a Boutique vacation experience, influenced by the laidback attitude and local flare of this African destination.

Rooms

Our rooms and suites is modern interpretations of architectural style. Layers of textures and historical patterns in earthy tones, decorative accessories by locals combine to create warm, sophisticated spaces with a residential feel.

Restaurant

Our exclusive poolside restaurant partner on the four Proper dining concepts. Indulge in Israeli & Turkish-inspired fare on the ground floor Peacock Mediterranean Grill as well as our on-the-go version, The Mockingbird with Coffee, Falafel and Rotisserie

Spa & Wellness

Only steps away from downtown’s buzz and waterfront, our holistic spa getaway offers a respite of physical and mental calm to balance Equity’s naturally active energy. Upon entry, decompress with a detoxifying drink in our pretreatment reception lounge.
